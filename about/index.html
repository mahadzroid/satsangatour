about us

The word Satsanga is derived from Sanskrit, meaning “association with the good” or “company of the truth.” At Satsanga Tours & Travel, we believe that the best travel does exactly that: it connects you with the good, the true, and the beautiful in the world.
We are more than just a travel agency; we are your companions on the path to discovery. We curate journeys that go beyond the standard tourist traps to offer a genuine connection with the soul of a destination. Whether you are seeking spiritual solace in the Himalayas, cultural immersion in ancient cities, or the simple truth of a breathtaking sunset, we are here to facilitate that experience

Our Mission

We are a team of avid explorers and logistics experts dedicated to crafting seamless and unforgettable adventures. We believe that travel should be enriching, authentic, and effortlessly organized

Our Values

We value real experiences over checklists. We connect you with the soul of a place.
Effortless Travel: We value your time and sanity. We handle the stress, you collect the memories.
Personalized Service: We value your uniqueness. Your trip is designed for you, not for a crowd.
